Have an Intention

It's of paramount importance to have an intention. You must know what you desire to move into.

This quality has often atrophied in many. They simply do not know what they want. That aspect of the self has been crippled.

To rebuild this quality, simply ask yourself these two questions.

"What do I want?" and "What do I not want?"

By hitting both sides, you'll move into alignment.

If emotions come up, what I refer to as a [[charge]] be sure to check out my meditation [[Harvesting Meditation|Harvesting]]

Using this meditation, you'll be able to consume and digest the discomfort that is creating the lack of clarity and direction.

Practice asking these questions throughout the day. Eventually, it will become habitual.
